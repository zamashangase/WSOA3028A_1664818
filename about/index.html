Hi I'm Nandi and this is what I want you to know

My full name is Nandipha Zulu, and yes I was named after one of South Africa's most famous Zulu Queens. I'm twenty four years old and I'm originally from Durban. I currently live in Joburg.

My Hobbies

I love different types of art. I'm a painter myself. If I'm not painting I'm usually listening music and reading a book. I'm a bit of a goth and dont like socializing that much. I love watering my plants and riding my motorcycle.

My Job

Well not many people know me in the city because I'm more of an indoor person but I'm an art consultant for a rich families here in Joburg. I usually spend time at the front of my laptop and get phone calls from work telling me what do I think about art pieces from different South African artists and whether any clients would be interested in these specific artworks. It's really fun since I get to look at different artworks and try and feel the depth and emotions through the paintings. But I hope these rich families see the beauty of these artworks instead of thinking of it as new house decorations but hey a girl's gotta get paid lol.
